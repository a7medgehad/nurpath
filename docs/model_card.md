# Model Card (MVP Draft)

## Purpose

Educational Islamic learning support in Arabic and English with citation-grounded outputs.

## Intended Use

- Topic exploration
- Comparative understanding of scholarly views
- Structured learning progression

## Out of Scope

- Personalized binding fatwas
- Legal/medical emergency advice

## Safety Controls

- Confidence gating and abstention
- Citation requirements
- Escalation notice for sensitive rulings
